Paragraphs and line breaks

Users of HTML are sometimes surprised to find that HTML gives them little control over the way that a page is displayed. It should be remembered that HTML was developed as a means of marking up the structure of a document not as a way of determining its presentation.

Formatting text to appear on a Web page is therefore different from formatting text to appear

Definitions of the four seasons

  • Spring -- Time of the year in which the weather becomes warmer and plants revive.
  • Summer -- The warmest season of the year
  • Fall-- Time of the year in which the weather becomes cooler and many plants become dormant.
  • Winter-- The coldest season of the year.
